# 🎤 InterviewMate (Under Development)

🚧 **Work in Progress** — InterviewMate is an AI-powered interview practice platform that simulates real interviews through voice-based, real-time conversations with AI interviewers.

It helps candidates practice HR, technical, and managerial interviews with instant feedback, analytics, and professional reports.

---

## Product Overview

**Name:** InterviewMate  
**Tagline:** Practice real interviews with AI. Perform with confidence.  
**Mission:** Help job seekers improve interview performance by providing realistic mock interviews, structured feedback, and measurable progress tracking.

---

## Current Status

- Product scope and feature set defined  
- System architecture and data models designed  
- Core interview flow implemented  
- Advanced AI evaluation and reporting in progress  
- Not production-ready  

---

## Problem

Job seekers often struggle to prepare effectively because:
- Mock interviews lack realism and adaptability  
- Feedback is generic or delayed  
- No structured way to track progress over time  
- Professional coaching is expensive and inaccessible  

---

## Solution

InterviewMate provides a realistic, AI-driven interview experience to:
- Simulate real interview conversations (voice-based)  
- Adapt questions dynamically based on responses  
- Generate detailed performance reports  
- Track improvement across multiple interview sessions  

---

## Key Features (Planned & In Progress)

- AI-powered mock interviews (HR, Technical, Managerial)  
- Voice-based real-time interview sessions  
- Adaptive questioning and cross-questioning  
- Resume and job description–aware interviews  
- AI-generated evaluation and scoring  
- Professional PDF interview reports  
- Interview history and performance tracking  
- Subscription-based minute usage system  

---


Design goals:
- Real-time interaction

---

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React.js + Tailwind CSS  
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express  
- **Database:** MongoDB Atlas  
- **AI Services:** Google Gemini AI  
- **Voice AI:** VAPI AI (Pro mode), Web Speech API (Lite mode)  
- **Auth:** JWT + Google OAuth  
- **Payments:** Razorpay  
- **Real-time:** WebSockets  
- **PDF Reports:** jsPDF
